Preplate

Privacy Policy

Overview

Preplate is a meal planning and grocery shopping app published by Cooper Industries. We take your privacy seriously and have built the app to keep your data on your devices and inside your iCloud account whenever possible. This page explains exactly what data the app handles and why.

Data We Collect

Your recipes, meal plans, shopping lists, household preferences, and macro targets are stored locally on your device using Apple's SwiftData framework. If you enable iCloud sync, that same data is mirrored to your private CloudKit database so it syncs across devices you sign into with the same Apple ID. We do not operate any servers and we do not have access to anything in your iCloud account.

Health Data

Preplate writes nutrition data — calories, protein, carbohydrates, and fat — to Apple Health when you tap "Log Meal." The app never reads from Apple Health and never transmits health data off your device. You can revoke this access at any time in iOS Settings → Health → Data Access & Devices → Preplate.

Advertising

The free tier of Preplate displays banner advertisements from Google AdMob. iOS will ask whether you want to allow tracking the first time the app needs it; if you decline, AdMob serves you non-personalized ads. Premium subscribers see no ads at all. For details on what data Google receives, see Google's advertising privacy policy.

In-App Purchases

Premium subscriptions and the lifetime upgrade are processed entirely by Apple via StoreKit. Apple handles your payment information; Cooper Industries never sees your billing details, payment method, or Apple ID.

Analytics

None. Preplate does not include any third-party analytics SDKs, crash reporters, or usage trackers. We do not log how you use the app, which screens you visit, or which features you tap.

Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or share your data with anyone, with one exception: the AdMob banners described above receive the standard request data Google needs to serve an ad. We do not share recipes, meal plans, shopping lists, or any other content you create.

Household Sharing

If you invite household members to share recipes, meal plans, and shopping lists, sync happens peer-to-peer through CloudKit using Apple's CKShare mechanism. Cooper Industries has no access to shared data — it travels directly between iCloud accounts you authorize.

Data Retention & Deletion

Deleting Preplate from your device removes all locally stored data. To remove iCloud-synced data, sign into iCloud at icloud.com or open iOS Settings → your name → iCloud → Manage Account Storage → Preplate → Delete.

Children's Privacy

Preplate is not directed to children under 13 and we do not knowingly collect data from anyone under 13.

LEAP

Privacy Policy

Overview

LEAP is a hand-drawn vertical jumper published by Cooper Industries. The game keeps your progress on your device. We do not operate servers and we never receive your personal data.

What Stays on Your Device

Your high score, collected stars, unlocked skins, and settings (tilt sensitivity, sound, haptics) are stored locally on your device. Deleting the app removes all of it.

Game Center

If you sign into Game Center, your score is submitted to Apple's leaderboard along with the Game Center identity you have chosen to display. This is handled entirely by Apple. You can opt out by declining Game Center sign-in; the game is fully playable without it.

Advertising

LEAP displays advertisements from Google AdMob, including an optional "watch an ad to continue" rescue. iOS will ask whether you want to allow tracking; if you decline, AdMob serves non-personalized ads. If you allow it, your device's advertising identifier (IDFA) is shared with Google for personalized advertising. Purchasing Remove Ads eliminates ads entirely. See Google's advertising privacy policy for details.

In-App Purchases

The Remove Ads purchase is processed entirely by Apple via StoreKit. Apple handles your payment information; Cooper Industries never sees your billing details, payment method, or Apple ID.

Analytics

None. LEAP contains no third-party analytics SDKs, crash reporters, or usage trackers. The only advertising technology in the game is Google AdMob, described above.

Children's Privacy

LEAP is rated 4+ and is suitable for all ages. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. Because the game shows third-party ads, we recommend parents review the ad settings on their device.

Data Retention & Deletion

Deleting LEAP removes all local game data. Game Center scores are managed by Apple — remove them via your Game Center account settings.

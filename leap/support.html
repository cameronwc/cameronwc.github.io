How to Play

Hold anywhere on the screen to charge your jump — the longer you hold, the more power. Tilt your phone to aim. Release to launch. Hop from platform to platform and climb as high as you can.

Common Questions

The tilt aim feels wrong.

Tilt is calibrated to how you were holding the phone when the run started. Open Settings and adjust tilt sensitivity, or start a new run holding the phone in your natural position.

How do I remove ads?

Open Settings and tap Remove Ads. It's a one-time purchase.

I bought Remove Ads but ads came back.

Open Settings and tap Restore Purchases, making sure you're signed into the same Apple ID used for the original purchase.

My score didn't appear on the leaderboard.

Leaderboards require Game Center. Check that you're signed in via iOS Settings → Game Center. Scores submitted while signed out aren't recorded.

How do I unlock skins?

Collect stars during runs and spend them in the Shop.
